
# 📬 Inbox Sweeper

 Inbox Sweeper is a Chrome extension that helps you declutter your Gmail inbox by automatically identifying newsletters, promotional emails, marketing subscriptions, shopping offers, mailing lists, and other recurring subscription-based emails.

Many online services, retailers, and websites continue sending promotional emails long after you've signed up. Over time these messages can overwhelm your inbox, making it difficult to find important emails.

Inbox Sweeper gives you a centralized dashboard where you can:

* View all subscription and promotional senders in one place
* Track unread and total email counts
* Read emails directly within the extension
* Unsubscribe from unwanted senders
* Bulk delete emails from specific subscriptions
* Quickly clean up years of inbox clutter
